Chancellor

Dr. Ravi Pachamuthu, The Chairman of the SRM Group oversees the organization's strategic growth and evolution. He actively participated in the establishment of a number of educational institutions under the SRM Group's banner, including Valliammai Society and SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The internationally famous SRM University is now regarded as one of the best private universities in India, thanks to his capable and visionary leadership.
