About the Statutory Committee

The Statutory Committee at SRM University Sonepat Delhi-NCR is responsible for providing oversight and guidance across various academic and administrative matters. Comprising faculty members from diverse departments, the committee ensures comprehensive representation and multidisciplinary perspectives in decision-making processes. The committee plays a crucial role in maintaining institutional standards, addressing challenges, and implementing strategic initiatives for the university's growth and development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Formulating and reviewing university policies and regulations
  • Ensuring compliance with UGC and regulatory requirements
  • Overseeing academic quality assurance measures
  • Addressing institutional challenges and proposing solutions
  • Advising on infrastructure development and resource allocation
  • Reviewing student welfare initiatives and support services
  • Facilitating inter-departmental coordination and collaboration
